Privacy Policy – SacraChat App

Effective Date: October 5, 2025

SacraChat (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) values your privacy and is committed to being transparent about how your information is collected, used, and stored. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

SacraChat collects information through the Memory System and user feedback:

     
  • Memory System:    
           
    • The app scans your messages to identify personal information such as your name, age, hobbies, and other details you voluntarily share.
      •      
    • This information is stored locally on your device and is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
      •      
    • Memories may be used to personalize your experience and responses from AI. You can manage and delete these memories at any time.
      •    
     
    •  
  • Feedback via Discord:    
           
    • Messages you submit for feedback purposes are sent to our Discord server.
      •      
    • Feedback is used to improve the app and its functionality.
      •      
    • We do not sell this information.
      •    
     
 

2. Optional Data Collection

SacraChat includes an optional feature to help improve the app:

     
  • Conversation Sharing (Optional, Disabled by Default):    
           
    • You can choose to share your conversations anonymously with the developer to improve response quality.
      •      
    • This feature is OFF by default and must be manually enabled in Settings.
      •      
    • Shared conversations are stored on a Discord server accessible only to the developer. While we do not sell this data, Discord may have access to it per their own privacy policy.
      •      
    • No personally identifying information is collected alongside shared conversations.
      •      
    • You can disable this feature at any time in Settings.
      •    
     

3. Use of AI Services

SacraChat uses two AI services depending on the settings:

     
  • Magisterium Insights Mode (On):    
           
    • Uses the Magisterium AI API for theological or Catholic faith-based questions.
      •      
    • Responses are generated in accordance with Magisterium AI’s privacy policy.
      •    
     
    •  
  • General Mode / Magisterium Insights Mode (Off):    
           
    • Uses the OpenAI API for general knowledge, homework help, and other non-theological topics.
      •      
    • Responses follow OpenAI’s privacy policy.
      •    
     

Note: The Memory System always interacts with the OpenAI API to personalize responses, even when Magisterium Insights is enabled.

4. Data Storage

     
  • All conversations, settings, and memories are stored locally on your device.
    •  
  • Your data is not stored in the cloud and remains on your device unless you enable optional conversation sharing.
    •  
  • Information is only sent to the respective AI API (OpenAI or Magisterium) for generating responses, then discarded by those services per their retention policies.
    •  
  • SacraChat does not sell your data to third parties.
    •  
  • If you enable optional conversation sharing, those messages are stored on a Discord server accessible only to the developer and Discord itself.

5. User Control

     
  • You can view and delete memories in the app at any time.
    •  
  • You can toggle Magisterium Insights on or off in settings.
    •  
  • You can stop sending feedback via Discord at any time.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure your data is safe. While stored locally and transmitted securely to AI services, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

7. Children’s Privacy

SacraChat is intended for teens and young users. If you are under 13 (or the age required by your local law), please ensure you have parental consent before using the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect improvements to the app or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes.
